## Troubleshooting

### Low Engagement Issues

**Problem:** Few comments/likes on posts

**Possible Causes:**
- Not asking questions in posts
- Posting at wrong times
- Content doesn't resonate
- Comments disabled accidentally

**Solutions:**
1. Add question to every post
2. Check post timing (Dashboard â†’ Analytics â†’ Best Times)
3. Poll fans: "What content do you want?"
4. Enable comments (Settings â†’ Privacy â†’ Comments)
5. Run contest: "Best comment wins!"

---

**Problem:** Fans don't respond to messages

**Possible Causes:**
- Generic, impersonal messages
- Too frequent messaging (seen as spam)
- Wrong timing
- Fans inactive

**Solutions:**
1. Personalize every message (use names)
2. Reduce message frequency
3. Send at peak activity times
4. Re-engage inactive fans with special offer

---

**Problem:** Decreasing engagement over time

**Possible Causes:**
- Content becoming stale
- Less interaction from you
- Fans losing interest
- Algorithm changes

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh content style
2. Increase your engagement with fans
3. Run special promotions
4. Survey fans: "How can I improve?"
5. Analyze what worked before, do more of it

---

- Don't feed negativity
- Focus on positive fans

**2. Polite Redirect**

- Use platform's content protection features
