## <a name="refunds"></a>â†©ï¸ Refunds & Chargebacks

### Refund Policy

**Standard Refund Window: 48 hours**

Fans can request refund within 48 hours if:
- Content not as described
- Technical issue prevented access
- Accidental purchase
- Unauthorized use of card

**Non-Refundable:**
- Subscriptions after 48 hours
- PPV content already viewed/downloaded
- Tips (considered voluntary gifts)
- Custom content (work already done)

### How Refunds Affect You

**When refund issued:**
1. Amount deducted from your balance
2. If already paid out, negative balance created
3. Negative balance deducted from future earnings
4. You receive notification

**Example:**
```
November 15 payout: $500 (sent to your bank)
November 20: Fan refunded $50

Your account:
- Balance: -$50
- Next payout (Nov 30): Earn $300
- Deduct negative: -$50
- Actual payout: $250
```

### Chargebacks

**What is a chargeback?**
- Fan disputes charge with credit card company
- Claims: Unauthorized, fraud, not as described
- Card company reverses charge
- More serious than refund

**Chargeback Process:**

1. **Fan files dispute** with their bank
2. **Bank notifies us** (2-8 weeks after purchase)
3. **We notify you** - Give you chance to respond
4. **You can contest** with evidence:
   - Screenshots showing delivery
   - Messages with fan
   - Proof of access/download
   - Terms they agreed to
5. **Bank decides** (30-90 days)
6. **Money returned or kept**

**Chargeback Fees:**
- $15 fee per chargeback (even if you win)
- Deducted from your earnings
- Industry standard, covers processing costs

**High Chargebacks = Problem:**
- >1% chargeback rate = Warning
- >3% chargeback rate = Account review
- >5% chargeback rate = Possible suspension

### Preventing Chargebacks

âœ“ **Clear descriptions:** Describe content accurately
âœ“ **Deliver value:** Ensure content matches expectations
âœ“ **Watermark content:** Prevents "I didn't get it" claims
âœ“ **Respond to messages:** Address issues before they escalate
âœ“ **Professional conduct:** Maintain positive relationships
âœ“ **Save evidence:** Keep records of all interactions

### Disputing a Chargeback

**If you believe chargeback is fraudulent:**

1. **Gather evidence:**
   - Screenshots of content delivery
   - Message history with fan
   - Access logs (we provide these)
   - Previous successful transactions
   - Terms and conditions they agreed to

2. **Submit dispute:**
   - Help â†’ Payment Issues â†’ Dispute Chargeback
   - Upload evidence
   - Write detailed explanation
   - Submit within 7 days

3. **We present to bank:**
   - Send your evidence to bank
   - Fight on your behalf
   - Follow up

4. **Decision:**
   - Win: Money returned to you, fee refunded
   - Loss: Charge remains reversed, fee stands
   - Partial: Rare, sometimes split amount

**Win rate:** ~40% of disputes are won when strong evidence provided

## <a name="faq"></a>â“ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Twice monthly - earnings from 1st-15th paid on the 15th, earnings from 16th-end paid on last day of month. Funds arrive in your bank 3-5 business days later.

**Q: What's the minimum payout?**

**Q: Can I change my payment method?**

**Q: Why was my payout less than expected?**

**Q: Are tips taxable income?**

**Q: Can fans tip anonymously?**
A: Fans can tip with a custom display name, but we maintain records for fraud prevention and tax reporting.
