## What are Stories?

**Stories** are temporary posts that disappear after 24 hours. They appear at the top of your profile and in a dedicated Stories feed.

**Key Differences from Regular Posts:**

| Feature | Regular Posts | Stories |
|---------|--------------|---------|
| **Duration** | Permanent | 24 hours |
| **Format** | Vertical or horizontal | Vertical (9:16 ratio) |
| **Location** | Main feed | Top of profile/Stories feed |
| **Engagement** | Likes, comments, shares | Views, reactions, replies |
| **Analytics** | Detailed stats | Views + engagement rate |
| **Editing** | Can edit anytime | Can't edit after posting |

## Creating Stories

### Basic Story Creation

**Step 1: Access Stories**

**Desktop:**
1. Click your profile icon at top of screen
2. Click **+ Add Story** button
3. Upload content or create new

**Mobile:**
1. Tap **Camera icon** (top left) or **Your story circle** (top of feed)
2. Take photo/video or select from gallery

---

**Step 2: Choose Content**

**Options:**

1. **Take photo** (camera icon)
   - Click capture button
   - Switch front/back camera
   - Use flash if needed

2. **Record video** (video icon)
   - Hold capture button (max 60 seconds)
   - Release to stop
   - Or tap for hands-free recording

3. **Upload from gallery**
   - Click gallery icon
   - Select photo or video
   - Multiple selections for multi-frame story

4. **Create text-only story**
   - Click "Aa" text icon
   - Choose background color
   - Type message
   - Adjust font and size

---

**Step 3: Edit & Enhance**

**Tools available:**

**Text:**
- Add text overlay
- Multiple fonts available
- Colored backgrounds
- Drag to reposition

**Stickers:**
- Emojis
- GIFs
- Location tags
- Timestamp
- Poll stickers
- Question stickers
- Quiz stickers

**Drawing:**
- Freehand drawing

### Benchmarking

**Average performance metrics:**

| Subscribers | Expected Views | Completion Rate | Replies |
|------------|---------------|-----------------|---------|
| 0-100 | 30-60% | 70-80% | 5-10% |
| 100-500 | 40-70% | 60-75% | 3-7% |
| 500-1000 | 50-80% | 50-70% | 2-5% |
| 1000-5000 | 30-60% | 40-60% | 1-3% |
| 5000+ | 20-40% | 30-50% | 1-2% |

**Why views drop with growth:**
- Not all subscribers check stories daily
- Larger audience = more passive followers
- Quality matters more than quantity

**Good performance indicators:**
- Views > 50% of subscriber count
- Completion rate > 60%
- Engagement (replies/reactions) > 5%

## Story Highlights

### What are Highlights?

**Story Highlights** preserve stories permanently on your profile.

**Why create highlights:**
- Save best stories beyond 24 hours
- Organize stories by topic/theme
- New subscribers see your best content
- Showcase personality and content style
- Create evergreen story content

**Where they appear:**
- Below bio on profile
- As circles with custom covers
- Fans tap to view saved stories

---

### Creating Highlights

**Step 1: Save to Highlight**

**During story creation:**
1. Before posting, toggle **Add to Highlight**
2. Choose existing highlight or create new
3. Post story
4. Automatically saved to highlight

**After story is live:**
1. Open your story
2. Tap **Highlight** button
3. Select or create highlight
4. Save

**Before story expires:**
1. Go to your profile
2. Tap story ring
3. Tap **Highlight** for any story frame
4. Add to highlight before 24-hour expiration

---

**Step 2: Organize Highlights**

**Create themed highlights:**

**Common themes:**
- **FAQ**: Frequently asked questions
- **Behind the Scenes**: BTS content
- **Tips**: Advice and tips for fans
- **Day in the Life**: Daily routines
- **Q&As**: Question and answer sessions
- **Throwbacks**: Old photos/memories
- **Announcements**: Important updates
- **Testimonials**: Fan feedback/reviews

**How to create:**
1. Tap **+ New** in highlights section
2. Name the highlight (e.g., "FAQ")
3. Choose cover image
4. Select stories to include
5. Save

---

**Step 3: Customize Highlight Covers**

**Make highlights visually appealing:**

**Option 1: Use Story Frame**
- Select any frame from stories in that highlight
- Becomes the cover thumbnail

**Option 2: Upload Custom Cover**
- Design cover image (1080x1920px recommended)
- Upload as cover
- Consistent branding across all highlights

**Design tips:**
- Use consistent color scheme
- Clear text labels
- Simple, recognizable icons
- Professional look

---

### Managing Highlights

**Edit Highlight:**
1. Tap and hold highlight
2. Select **Edit Highlight**
3. Options:
   - Rename
   - Change cover
   - Add more stories
   - Remove stories
   - Reorder stories

**Delete Highlight:**
1. Tap and hold highlight
2. Select **Delete Highlight**
3. Confirm deletion
4. Stories return to archive (not deleted permanently)

**Reorder Highlights:**
- Tap and hold highlight
- Drag to new position
- Order appears on profile

## Story Strategy & Best Practices

### Posting Frequency

**Recommended:**
- **1-3 stories per day** for most creators
- **3-5 stories per day** if very active
- **5-10 stories per day** during special events

**Why not more:**
- Overwhelming subscribers
- Lower completion rates
- Diminishing returns
- Viewer fatigue

**Timing strategy:**
- **Morning** (7-9 AM): Good morning, coffee content
- **Afternoon** (12-2 PM): Lunch break browsing
- **Evening** (6-9 PM): Peak engagement time
- **Night** (9-11 PM): Before bed scrolling

**Spread throughout day** for maximum reach.

## Troubleshooting

### Story Won't Upload

**Common issues:**

**1. File size too large**
- Videos: Max 500 MB
- Photos: Max 50 MB
- Solution: Compress before uploading

**2. Unsupported format**
- Supported: JPG, PNG, MP4, MOV
- Solution: Convert to supported format

**3. Poor internet connection**
- Solution: Switch to WiFi or better signal
- Wait for upload to complete

**4. App glitch**
- Solution: Close and reopen app
- Update to latest version
- Clear cache

**5. Account restriction**
- Check for policy violations
- Verify account in good standing

---

### Story Not Appearing

**If your story posted but doesn't show:**

**Check:**
1. Privacy settings (might be too restricted)
2. Subscribers' settings (they may have muted your stories)
3. Story order (might be behind other stories in feed)
4. Time - give it a few minutes to propagate

**Force refresh:**
- Close app completely
- Reopen
- Check if story appears

---

### Low Story Views

**Reasons:**

**1. Timing**
- Posted at low-activity time
- Solution: Post during peak hours

**2. Content quality**
- Boring or unclear content
- Solution: Make first frame eye-catching

**3. Posting too frequently**
- Viewer fatigue
- Solution: Reduce to 1-3 per day

**4. Low subscriber engagement overall**
- Inactive subscribers
- Solution: Engage more, create better content, run promotions

**5. Not using interactive features**
- Passive stories get lower engagement
- Solution: Add polls, questions, stickers

---

### Interactive Stickers Not Working

**Poll/quiz/question sticker issues:**

**Problem:** Sticker not responding

**Solutions:**
1. Update app to latest version
2. Restart app
3. Try different sticker type
4. Check if account verified (some features require verification)

**Problem:** Not receiving responses

**Solutions:**
1. Check that sticker is properly placed
2. Ensure story privacy allows interaction
3. Wait - responses may be delayed
4. Verify in insights section

---

### Story Quality Issues

**Video appears pixelated or low quality:**

**Causes:**
- Uploaded low-resolution source
- Compressed too much
- Wrong aspect ratio (stretched)

**Solutions:**
1. Upload highest resolution possible
2. Use 9:16 aspect ratio (1080x1920px ideal)
3. Shoot in good lighting
4. Use platform's camera instead of uploading (sometimes better quality)

---

### Can't Add Music

**Music sticker unavailable:**

**Reasons:**
1. Copyright restrictions in your region
2. Account not verified
3. Music feature not enabled
4. App version outdated

**Solutions:**
- Verify account
- Update app
- Check regional availability
- Use platform-approved music library only
