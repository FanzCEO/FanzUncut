## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Upload failed - Error 500"

**Cause:** File too large, unsupported format, or server issue

**Solution:**
1. Check file size (max 5GB for videos, 50MB for photos)
2. Verify file format (MP4, JPG, PNG only)
3. Try compressing the file
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser
6. Check internet connection (upload speed minimum 5 Mbps)

**Still not working?**
- Try uploading from mobile app
- Contact support with error code

---

### Problem: "Content stuck in moderation for 24+ hours"

**Cause:** Flagged for manual review or high moderation queue volume

**Solution:**
1. Wait up to 48 hours for manual review
2. Check for policy violations
3. Ensure all co-stars are verified
4. Avoid restricted content (see content policy)

**If still pending after 48 hours:**
- Submit support ticket with content ID
- Include upload timestamp
- Response within 2-4 hours

---

### Problem: "Low quality warning"

**Cause:** Resolution too low, excessive compression, or poor lighting

**Solution:**
1. **Photos:** Use minimum 1080x1080 resolution
2. **Videos:** Minimum 720p (1080p recommended)
3. Improve lighting when shooting
4. Use less aggressive compression
5. Avoid excessive filters/editing

**Quality tips:**
- Shoot in well-lit environments
- Use your phone's highest quality setting
- Avoid digital zoom
- Clean your camera lens

---

### Problem: "Can't add thumbnail to video"

**Cause:** Browser compatibility or file format issue

**Solution:**
1. Use Chrome or Firefox (best compatibility)
2. Ensure thumbnail is JPG or PNG
3. Keep thumbnail under 5MB
4. Use 1920x1080 resolution
5. Clear cache and try again

**Workaround:**
- Upload video first
- Edit after approval
- Add thumbnail from media library

---

### Problem: "Tags not saving"

**Cause:** Special characters, tag limit exceeded, or browser issue

**Solution:**
1. Remove special characters from tags
2. Limit to 10 tags maximum
3. Press Enter after each tag
4. Use single words or hyphenated phrases
5. Refresh page and try again

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does moderation take?
**A:** Typically 2-4 hours. During peak times (evenings, weekends), it may take up to 24 hours. Content flagged for manual review can take up to 48 hours. You'll receive a notification when your content is approved or if it needs changes.

### Q: Can I edit content after publishing?
**A:** Yes! Go to your Media Library, find the content, and click "Edit". You can change title, description, tags, and pricing. Visual content (the photo/video itself) cannot be changed - you'll need to delete and re-upload.

### Q: What's the best content to charge for (PPV)?
**A:** Reserve PPV for your most exclusive, high-value content: professional photoshoots, long-form videos (10+ minutes), collaborations, or custom requests. Keep 70% of content for subscribers to maintain value of subscription. PPV content typically earns 2-3x more than subscriber-only content.

### Q: How many times per week should I post?
**A:** Aim for 3-5 posts per week minimum. Top earners post 5-7 times weekly. Consistency matters more than volume - posting 3x/week regularly is better than 7x one week and 0x the next. Use stories daily to maintain presence between posts.

### Q: Why was my content rejected?
**A:** Common reasons: policy violations, unclear age verification, missing co-star verification, prohibited content, copyright infringement, or quality issues. Check your rejection notice for specific reason and guidance. Contact support if unclear.

### Q: Can I schedule posts in advance?
**A:** Yes! After filling out all details, click "Schedule" instead of "Publish". Choose date and time (up to 30 days in advance). Scheduled content posts automatically and goes through moderation at scheduled time, not when scheduled.

### Q: What happens to earnings from deleted content?
**A:** You keep all earnings generated before deletion. Any active PPV access or subscriptions remain valid until expiration. Deleted content is removed from your profile immediately but fans who already purchased PPV can access it for 30 days.

### Q: Should I watermark my content?
**A:** Highly recommended! Watermarks deter theft and help identify your content if reposted elsewhere. Use semi-transparent watermarks in multiple locations. Enable auto-watermarking in Settings > Content Protection.

### Q: How do I know what content performs best?
**A:** Check Analytics in your dashboard. Look for:
- Views and engagement rate
- Subscriber conversion rate
- Revenue per post
- Peak viewing times
- Top-performing tags and categories

### Q: Can fans download my content?
**A:** No, platform prevents downloads by default. Content protection includes:
- Download prevention
- Screenshot detection (watermark added)
- Right-click disabling
- Encrypted streaming for videos
