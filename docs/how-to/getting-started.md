## Identity Verification

**Required for:** Receiving payments

**Why Required:**
- Confirms you're 18+
- Prevents fraud
- Legal compliance
- Required for payouts

### Step 1: Navigate to Verification

Settings ‚Üí **Verification** ‚Üí **Verify Identity**

### Step 2: Prepare Documents

**You'll Need:**
- Government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
- Smartphone or webcam for selfie
- Well-lit area

### Step 3: Upload ID

**Front of ID:**
1. Click **Upload ID**
2. Take clear photo of ID
3. Ensure all corners visible
4. No glare or shadows
5. Text readable

**Important:**
- ID must be current (not expired)
- Name on ID must match your account
- All information clearly visible

### Step 4: Take Selfie

**Selfie with ID:**
1. Hold ID next to your face
2. Take selfie showing both your face and ID clearly
3. Good lighting
4. Face and ID both in focus

### Step 5: Submit for Review

**Click "Submit for Verification"**

**Processing Time:** 1-3 business days

**You'll Receive Email:**
- ‚úÖ Verified: "Your account is verified!"
- ‚ùå Rejected: "Verification rejected - [reason]"

### If Rejected

**Common Reasons:**
- ID photo not clear/readable
- Selfie doesn't match ID
- ID is expired
- Poor lighting/quality

**Solution:**
- Review rejection reason in email
- Fix the issue
- Resubmit with clearer photos
- Can reapply immediately

**Need Help?**

## Best Practices for New Creators

### Content Strategy

**1. Consistency Over Quality (At First)**
- Better to post 5 good posts/week than 1 perfect post/week
- Fans subscribe for regular content
- You'll improve quality over time
- Maintain schedule

**2. Mix Content Types**
- 60% main content (polished photos/videos)
- 30% casual content (selfies, updates)
- 10% engagement content (polls, Q&A)

**3. Behind-the-Scenes**
- Fans love authentic, personal content
- Show your process
- Share your day
- Be relatable

### Engagement Strategy

**1. Respond to Everything**
- Every comment
- Every message
- Within 24 hours
- Builds loyalty

**2. Use Names**
- Personalize responses
- "Thanks Sarah!"
- Makes fans feel valued
- Creates connection

**3. Ask Questions**
- End every post with a question
- Increases comments 3-5x
- Builds engagement
- Learn what fans want

### Growth Strategy

**1. Social Media Promotion**
- Daily teaser posts
- Consistent messaging
- Call-to-action always
- Link in every bio

**2. Leverage Free Posts**
- 20-30% of posts should be free (public)
- Teasers of subscriber content
- Shows value proposition
- Drives subscriptions

**3. Engage First 30 Days Heavily**
- Critical period
- High engagement = high retention
- Personal messages
- Build relationships

### Pricing Strategy

**1. Start Lower**
- $4.99-$7.99 first month
- Build subscriber base
- Prove value
- Raise later

**2. Gradual Increases**
- Raise $2-$3 every 3 months
- Grandfather existing subscribers (loyalty)
- New subscribers pay new price
- Communicate changes

**3. Test PPV Pricing**
- Start low ($5-$10)
- See what sells
- Adjust based on data
- Premium content = higher price

## Common Beginner Mistakes

### Mistake #1: Inconsistent Posting

**Problem:**
- Post 10 times week 1
- 2 times week 2
- Nothing week 3
- Fans lose interest

**Solution:**
- Set sustainable schedule
- Use post scheduler
- Batch create content
- 5 posts/week minimum

### Mistake #2: Pricing Too High Too Soon

**Problem:**
- $19.99/month, zero posts
- No social proof
- Hard to get subscribers
- Slow growth

**Solution:**
- Start at $4.99-$7.99
- Build to 50-100 subscribers
- Raise gradually
- Earn trust first

### Mistake #3: Ignoring Engagement

**Problem:**
- Post and ghost
- Don't respond to comments
- Don't send messages
- Fans feel ignored

**Solution:**
- Respond to ALL comments (80%+ target)
- Send welcome message to new subscribers
- Personal thank-yous
- Build relationships

### Mistake #4: Too Much PPV, Not Enough Free

**Problem:**
- Everything is pay-per-view
- Subscription feels like paywall
- Fans frustrated
- High churn

**Solution:**
- 80% included in subscription
- 20% PPV premium content
- Balance value
- Don't nickel-and-dime

### Mistake #5: Poor Profile Setup

**Problem:**
- No profile photo
- Empty bio
- No banner
- Looks unfinished

**Solution:**
- Complete ALL profile sections
- Professional appearance
- Clear value proposition
- First impressions matter

### Mistake #6: Not Promoting

**Problem:**
- "Build it and they will come"
- Wait for subscribers
- No social media presence
- Minimal growth

**Solution:**
- Active promotion daily
- All social platforms
- Teasers and previews
- Engage in communities

### Mistake #7: Quitting Too Soon

**Problem:**
- Expect overnight success
- Quit after 2 weeks
- "It's not working"
- Give up

**Solution:**
- Give it 90 days minimum
- Consistent effort required
- Growth compounds
- Patience pays off
