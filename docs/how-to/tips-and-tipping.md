## What are Tips?

**Tips** are voluntary payments fans can send to creators to show appreciation, support, or make special requests. Tips are **in addition to** subscription fees.

**Key Differences:**

| Feature | Subscription | Tip |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Frequency** | Recurring monthly | One-time payment |
| **Purpose** | Access to content | Appreciation/requests |
| **Amount** | Fixed price | Fan chooses amount |
| **Content access** | Unlocks subscriber content | Optional reward/response |
| **Required** | Yes (to see content) | No (voluntary) |

**Why Tips Matter:**

- **For Creators:**
  - Additional income beyond subscriptions
  - Direct appreciation from fans
  - Revenue from non-subscribers
  - Funding for special content or requests
  - Average 15-30% of total creator income

- **For Fans:**
  - Show appreciation for specific content
  - Support favorite creators
  - Get creator's attention
  - Request custom content or responses
  - Stand out in creator's DMs

## Sending Tips (For Fans)

### How to Send a Tip

**Method 1: Tip on a Post**

1. View any post
2. Click **Tip** button (üí∞ icon)
3. Choose amount:
   - Click suggested amount ($5, $10, $20, $50)
   - Or enter custom amount
4. Add optional message (visible to creator only)
5. Click **Send Tip**
6. Confirm payment

**Method 2: Tip in DMs**

1. Open conversation with creator
2. Click **Tip** button in message toolbar
3. Select amount
4. Add message (optional)
5. Send tip

**Method 3: Tip on Profile**

1. Go to creator's profile
2. Click **Send Tip** button (below profile pic)
3. Choose amount and message
4. Send

**Method 4: Tip During Live Stream**

1. During live stream, click **Tip** button
2. Select amount
3. Add message (shows in live chat if you choose)
4. Send tip
5. Tip appears in stream alerts (if creator enabled)

---

### Anonymous Tipping

**Can I tip anonymously?**

**Partial anonymity:**
- Creator always sees who tipped (for safety/compliance)
- Other fans don't see who tipped
- Tips don't appear in public activity feed

**How to tip privately:**
1. Send tip as normal
2. Uncheck **"Show in public activity"**
3. Tip won't appear on your profile's activity
4. Creator still sees your username

**Note:** Platform doesn't offer fully anonymous tips (regulatory compliance).

## Tip Menus & Requests

### What is a Tip Menu?

A **tip menu** lists specific actions or content fans can request by sending the corresponding tip amount.

**Example tip menu:**

| Tip Amount | Reward |
|-----------|--------|
| **$5** | Personalized thank you message |
| **$10** | Rate your profile 1-10 + feedback |
| **$15** | Exclusive behind-the-scenes photo |
| **$25** | Custom photo (your outfit choice) |
| **$50** | Custom video (30-60 seconds, your theme) |
| **$100** | 10-minute video call |
| **$200** | Custom photoshoot (your concept, 10+ photos) |
| **$500** | Exclusive content series (5 videos over a month) |

---

### Creating Your Tip Menu

**Step 1: Go to Tip Menu Settings**

1. **Dashboard ‚Üí Monetization ‚Üí Tip Menu**
2. Click **Create Tip Menu**

**Step 2: Add Menu Items**

For each item:
1. Click **Add Item**
2. Fill in:
   - **Tip amount:** $25
   - **Item name:** "Custom photo"
   - **Description:** "I'll create a custom photo wearing outfit of your choice. Delivered within 3 days."
   - **Delivery time:** "2-3 days"
   - **Availability:** "Unlimited" or "Limited (5 per month)"
3. Upload preview image (optional but recommended)
4. Click **Save Item**

**Step 3: Organize Menu**

- Drag items to reorder (cheapest to most expensive recommended)
- Toggle items ON/OFF (pause without deleting)
- Set monthly limits on time-intensive items

**Step 4: Publish Menu**

1. Click **Publish Menu**
2. Menu appears on your profile under "Tip Menu" tab
3. Fans can browse and send tips for specific items

### Fulfilling Tip Menu Requests

**Tracking requests:**

1. **Dashboard ‚Üí Tip Menu ‚Üí Requests**

   - Item requested

   - Special instructions

**Fulfillment workflow:**

1. **Acknowledge** immediately: "Got your request! I'll have this ready for you by [date]."
2. **Create content** according to request
3. **Deliver via DM**: Send the custom content in direct message
4. **Mark complete**: Dashboard ‚Üí Requests ‚Üí Mark as Fulfilled
5. **Follow up**: "Hope you love it! Let me know if you'd like anything else! üòä"

**Important:**
- Fulfill within promised timeframe

### Tip Menu Best Practices

**Pricing strategy:**

- **Low entry point** ($5-10): Easy, quick items to encourage first-time tippers
- **Mid-tier** ($25-75): Custom content requiring moderate effort
- **Premium** ($100-500): Time-intensive, highly personalized content

**Clear descriptions:**
- Specify exactly what fan receives
- Include delivery timeframe
- Note any limitations or requirements
- Set expectations (photo count, video length, etc.)

**Examples of clear vs. unclear:**

‚ùå **Unclear:** "$50 - Custom video"
‚úÖ **Clear:** "$50 - Custom 1-minute video, your theme choice, wearing outfit you choose. Delivered within 5 days. SFW requests only."

**Smart limits:**
- Limit time-intensive items (e.g., "Max 3 video calls per month")
- Pause items when backlogged
- Increase prices if overwhelmed with requests
- Block unreasonable requests (violate ToS, too demanding)

## Tax & Financial Considerations

### Tax Reporting

**In the United States:**

**1099-NEC Form:**
- Platform sends 1099 if you earn $600+ annually
- Reports all income including tips
- Due in January for previous year

**Your responsibility:**
- Report ALL tip income on tax return
- Even if under $600 threshold
- Include in self-employment income
- Pay quarterly estimated taxes if earning $1000+ annually

**Deductible expenses:**
- Equipment (cameras, lighting, computer)
- Internet service
- Content creation costs (outfits, props, location rentals)
- Software subscriptions (editing, design)
- Home office (if qualified)

**Consult a tax professional** for specific guidance.

---

### International Creators

**Currency conversion:**
- Tips received in USD (or local platform currency)
- Converted to your local currency upon withdrawal
- Exchange rates apply
- Conversion fees may apply (typically 2-3%)

**Tax obligations:**
- Varies by country
- Report all income to local tax authority
- May need to register as self-employed
- VAT/GST implications in some countries

**Payment restrictions:**
- Some countries restricted by platform
- Verify your country is supported: Settings ‚Üí Payments ‚Üí Supported Countries

---

### Financial Planning

**Tip income is unpredictable:**
- Can fluctuate 50-200% month to month
- Don't rely on tips for fixed expenses
- Treat as bonus income
- Save 25-35% for taxes

**Budgeting tips:**
- Track monthly tip averages
- Calculate conservative baseline (lowest 3 months average)
- Plan major purchases around busy months
- Build emergency fund with tip income

## Troubleshooting

### Fan Can't Send Tip

**Common issues:**

**1. Payment method declined**
- Card expired or insufficient funds
- Bank blocking transaction (flag as safe)
- Daily spending limit reached
- Card doesn't support international transactions (if platform is international)

**Solution:** Try different payment method or contact bank

**2. Tipping disabled on content**
- Creator disabled tips on that specific post
- Creator's account under review
- Content flagged (tips auto-disabled during review)

**Solution:** Tip via profile or DM instead

**3. Account restrictions**
- Fan account too new (anti-fraud measure)
- Previous payment disputes/chargebacks
- Location restrictions

**Solution:** Contact support or wait 7-14 days for new accounts

---

### Creator Not Receiving Tips

**Check these:**

**1. Payment account setup**
- Go to Settings ‚Üí Payments
- Verify bank account connected and verified
- Ensure no pending verification requirements

**2. Tips enabled**
- Settings ‚Üí Monetization ‚Üí Tips
- Verify "Enable Tips" is toggled ON
- Check that all tip locations are enabled

**3. Account verification**
- Complete identity verification
- Age verification confirmed
- No account restrictions

**4. Pending review**
- If content under review, tips may be held
- Resolve any policy issues
- Contact support for status

---

### Tip Disputes & Refunds

**When fans request refunds:**

**Legitimate reasons:**
- Duplicate charge (technical error)
- Creator didn't fulfill tip menu item
- Creator account suspended/deleted

**How to issue refund:**

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Tips ‚Üí Tip History**
2. Find the tip transaction
3. Click **Issue Refund**
4. Confirm refund
5. Funds return to fan within 5-7 business days

**Disputed tips:**
- Fan claims unauthorized charge
- Payment processor investigates
- You may need to provide proof of service
- Funds held during investigation

**Protect yourself:**
- Keep records of all tip menu fulfillments
- Screenshot custom content delivery
- Save all DM conversations
- Document completion of requests

---

### Tax Form Issues

**Didn't receive 1099:**
- Should arrive by January 31
- Check email (sent electronically)
- Download from Settings ‚Üí Tax Documents
- Contact support if missing

**1099 amount seems wrong:**
- Verify against your transaction history
- Download transaction report: Dashboard ‚Üí Reports ‚Üí Annual Income
- Contact support if discrepancy found

**Need to update tax information:**
- Settings ‚Üí Payments ‚Üí Tax Information
- Update SSN/EIN, address, legal name
- Changes apply to following tax year

---

### Payment Processing Delays

**Normal processing:**
- Tips appear in account balance instantly
- Withdrawal to bank: 2-5 business days
- First withdrawal may take 5-7 days (verification)

**Delays beyond normal:**
- Banking holidays (no processing)
- Bank account verification pending
- Large tip flagged for fraud review (automatic security measure)
- Payment account details incorrect

**Solution:**
1. Check Settings ‚Üí Payments ‚Üí Transaction Status
2. Verify bank details correct
3. Contact support if delayed 7+ business days
