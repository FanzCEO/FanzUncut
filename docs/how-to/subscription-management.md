## <a name="understanding"></a>üí° Understanding Subscriptions

### What are Subscriptions?

Subscriptions are **monthly recurring payments** from fans for exclusive access to your content.

**How it works:**
1. Fan pays monthly subscription fee (e.g., $9.99/month)
2. They get access to subscriber-only content
3. They're automatically charged each month
4. You earn **80% of subscription revenue**
5. They can cancel anytime

**Example:**
```
You set price: $9.99/month
Fan subscribes: Charged $9.99 on day 1
You earn: $7.99 (80%)
Platform: $2.00 (20%)

Day 30: Fan auto-charged $9.99 again
Day 60: Fan auto-charged $9.99 again
...continues until they cancel
```

### Subscription vs One-Time Purchases

| Subscription | PPV/Tips |
|-------------|----------|
| **Recurring** monthly revenue | **One-time** payment |
| **Predictable** income | **Variable** income |
| Access to **all** subscriber content | Access to **specific** post only |
| **Builds** long-term fans | **Attracts** casual viewers |
| **Stable** earnings | **Fluctuating** earnings |

**Best strategy:** Combine both!
- Subscriptions = reliable base income
- PPV/Tips = bonus earnings

### What Subscribers Get

**Included with subscription:**
- ‚úÖ All subscriber-only posts
- ‚úÖ Subscriber-only live streams
- ‚úÖ Direct messaging access (if enabled)
- ‚úÖ Exclusive content library
- ‚úÖ Behind-the-scenes updates
- ‚úÖ Community access

**NOT included (charge separately):**
- üí∞ PPV content (individual posts priced separately)
- üí∞ Custom requests
- üí∞ Tips
- üí∞ Private streams (can charge extra)
- üí∞ Physical items

## <a name="pricing"></a>üí∞ Setting Your Subscription Price

### Price Ranges

**New Creator (0-100 subscribers):**
- **Recommended:** $4.99 - $7.99/month
- Low barrier to entry
- Build subscriber base
- Prove your value
- Increase as you grow

**Established Creator (100-500 subscribers):**
- **Recommended:** $9.99 - $14.99/month
- Sweet spot for most creators
- Proven content value
- Loyal fanbase
- Sustainable earnings

**Premium Creator (500+ subscribers):**
- **Recommended:** $14.99 - $29.99/month
- High-quality exclusive content
- Strong brand/following
- Niche audience willing to pay
- Premium experience

**Elite/Celebrity Creator:**
- **Recommended:** $29.99 - $49.99/month
- Massive following
- Unique/exclusive access
- Limited availability
- Ultra-premium content

### How to Choose Your Price

**Consider these factors:**

**1. Content Quality**
- Professional photos/videos = higher price
- Phone selfies = lower price
- Production value matters

**2. Posting Frequency**
- Daily posts = $14.99+
- 3-5x per week = $9.99
- 1-2x per week = $4.99-$7.99
- Consistency is key!

**3. Your Niche**
- Unique/rare content = higher price
- Common content = competitive price
- Specialized fetish = premium pricing

**4. Competitor Pricing**
- Research similar creators
- Don't undervalue yourself
- Don't overprice without justification

**5. Your Following**
- Large following = can charge more
- Small following = start lower, build up
- Loyal fans = willing to pay premium

## <a name="faq"></a>‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I change my subscription price?**
A: Yes! Existing subscribers keep their current price. Only new subscribers pay the new price.

**Q: What percentage do I earn from subscriptions?**
A: You earn 80% of all subscription revenue. Platform takes 20%.

**Q: When do I get paid for subscriptions?**
A: Twice monthly (1st-15th paid on 15th, 16th-end paid on last day of month). Funds arrive in your bank 3-5 days later.

**Q: Can subscribers cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, but they keep access until the end of their current billing period (they already paid for that month).

**Q: What's a good subscriber count to aim for?**
A: Depends on your goals. 100 subs √ó $9.99 = $999/month ($799 to you). 500 subs = $3,995/month to you.

**Q: Should I offer free trials?**
A: Yes! Free trials increase conversions 2-3x. 7-14 days is optimal for most creators.

**Q: How do I reduce churn?**
A: Post consistently (3-5x/week), deliver quality, engage personally, and listen to feedback. Target <10% monthly churn.

**Q: Can I have multiple subscription tiers?**
A: Yes! Offer Basic, Premium, and VIP tiers at different price points with different perks.

**Q: What if someone subscribes just to download all my content then cancels?**
A: Watermark your content. They still paid for that month. If they re-share content, report DMCA violation.

**Q: Should I post all my content for subscribers only?**
A: No! Mix 70% public (teasers) and 30% subscriber-only. Public content attracts new subscribers.
