## Understanding Custom Requests

### What Are Custom Requests?

Custom requests allow fans to pay you to create specific content tailored to their preferences.

**How It Works:**

1. **Fan Submits Request**: Fan describes what they want and their budget
2. **You Review**: You receive notification of the request
3. **Accept or Counter**: Accept their offer, decline, or counter with your price
4. **Fan Pays**: Payment is held in escrow
5. **You Create**: Deliver the content within agreed timeframe
6. **Release Payment**: Once delivered, payment is released to you

**Revenue Split:**
- **You receive**: 80% of the custom request price
- **Platform fee**: 20%

### Types of Custom Requests

**1. Photo Sets**
- Specific outfits, poses, or themes
- Location-based requests
- Styled or themed shoots
- Typically: $25-$200+

**2. Videos**
- Personalized messages
- Specific scenarios or roleplay
- Custom storylines
- Typically: $50-$500+

**3. Messages**
- Personalized text messages
- Voice messages
- Video messages
- Typically: $10-$100+

**4. Other Content**
- Audio content
- Digital art
- Written content
- Custom pricing

## Managing Custom Requests

### Enabling Custom Requests

**Step 1: Turn On Custom Requests**

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Monetization** ‚Üí **Custom Requests**
2. Toggle **Accept Custom Requests** to ON
3. Set your availability status

**Step 2: Set Your Preferences**

```
Base Pricing:
- Minimum request price: $___
- Photo set base price: $___
- Video base price (per minute): $___
- Message base price: $___

Delivery Time:
- Standard delivery: 3-7 days
- Rush delivery option: +50% fee
- Express delivery: +100% fee

Content Guidelines:
- What you WILL create
- What you WON'T create
- Any special requirements
```

**Step 3: Set Boundaries**

Create clear guidelines about what you will and won't do:

**Will Create:**
- Specific outfits or styles
- Themed content
- Personalized messages
- Name mentions (at premium price)

**Won't Create:**
- [List your hard boundaries]
- [Anything that makes you uncomfortable]
- [Content that violates platform rules]

### Reviewing Incoming Requests

**Dashboard ‚Üí Custom Requests ‚Üí Pending**

You'll see:
- Fan's username and subscriber status
- Request description
- Their offered budget
- Requested delivery timeframe

**Your Options:**

1. **Accept** - Agree to their terms and price
2. **Counter** - Propose different price or timeline
3. **Decline** - Politely refuse the request
4. **Ask Questions** - Clarify details before deciding

### Accepting a Request

When you accept:

1. **Payment Goes to Escrow**: Platform holds payment securely
2. **Deadline Set**: Countdown starts for delivery
3. **You Get Notified**: Email and in-app notification
4. **Create Content**: Work on the custom content
5. **Upload Delivery**: Submit completed content
6. **Fan Reviews**: Fan has 48 hours to approve
7. **Payment Released**: Funds move to your account

**Important:**
- Missing deadlines can result in refunds
- Set realistic delivery times
- Communicate if you need more time

### Negotiating Price

**How to Counter an Offer:**

1. Click **Counter Offer** on the request
2. Enter your proposed price
3. Explain your pricing (optional but recommended)
4. Set your delivery timeline
5. Submit counter

**Pricing Tips:**

- Be reasonable but value your time
- More complex = higher price
- Rush requests = premium pricing
- Loyal subscribers = occasional discount

**Example Counter Message:**
> "Thanks for your request! For a 5-minute custom video with the specific outfit and script you mentioned, my rate is $150. I can have this ready for you within 5 days. Let me know if that works for you! üíï"

### Declining Requests

**When to Decline:**

- Request is outside your comfort zone
- Price too low for time/effort required
- Request violates platform rules
- You're at capacity

**How to Decline Politely:**

1. Click **Decline** on the request
2. Select reason (optional)
3. Add personal message (recommended)

**Example Decline Message:**
> "Thank you so much for thinking of me! Unfortunately, this type of content isn't something I create. I'd love to work with you on something else though - feel free to send another request!"

## Creating Custom Content

### Workflow Process

**1. Confirm Details (Day 0)**

After accepting request:
- Review all requirements carefully
- Ask clarifying questions immediately
- Confirm you have everything needed
- Set your production schedule

**2. Plan Your Shoot (Day 1-2)**

- Gather outfits, props, locations
- Write script or shot list
- Schedule your shooting time
- Prepare lighting/setup

**3. Create Content (Day 3-4)**

- Follow request requirements exactly
- Capture extra footage/photos (backup)
- Review as you go
- Ensure quality meets your standards

**4. Edit & Review (Day 5)**

- Edit video or select best photos
- Add any requested elements
- Quality check everything
- Have content ready ahead of deadline

**5. Deliver (Day 5-6)**

- Upload to custom request section
- Include all promised deliverables
- Add brief message
- Mark as delivered

**6. Follow Up (Day 7)**

- Check if fan approved
- Respond to any feedback
- Thank them for the request
- Invite future requests

### Quality Standards

**Maintain High Quality:**

Even though it's custom and pre-paid, quality matters for:
- Repeat business from same fan
- Reputation for custom work
- Reviews and ratings
- Future request volume

**Quality Checklist:**
- ‚úÖ Good lighting
- ‚úÖ Clear audio (for videos)
- ‚úÖ In-focus shots
- ‚úÖ Meets all stated requirements

- ‚úÖ Delivered on time

Set clear terms upfront:

## Common Issues

### Problem: Too Many Requests

**Issue**: Overwhelmed with custom request volume

**Solutions:**
1. Raise your prices (reduces volume, increases profit)
2. Limit concurrent requests (max 3 at a time)
3. Close requests periodically (open/close schedule)
4. Increase delivery timeframes (7-14 days)

### Problem: Lowball Offers

**Issue**: Fans offering unreasonably low prices

**Solutions:**
1. Set minimum request price (blocks low offers)
2. Post your starting rates publicly
3. Use counter offer with explanation
4. Decline and provide rate sheet

**Template Response:**
> "Thanks for your interest! My base rate for custom videos is $100 for 3 minutes. I'd be happy to create this for you at that rate. Let me know!"

### Problem: Unclear Requests

**Issue**: Request is vague or confusing

**Solutions:**
1. Ask specific questions before accepting
2. Request detailed description
3. Offer to jump on brief call/message
4. Create questionnaire for common requests

**Questions to Ask:**
- What specific outfits/props?
- What location/setting?
- What should I say/do?
- Any names to mention?
- Preferred length?
- Hard deadline?

### Problem: Scope Creep

**Issue**: Fan keeps adding requirements after you accepted

**Solutions:**
1. Refer to original request description
2. Offer to create additional content for added fee
3. Set clear revision policy upfront
4. Use contracts for high-value requests ($500+)

**Template Response:**
> "I'm happy to create what we originally discussed. The new elements you mentioned would be a separate request. I can do both - let me know if you'd like me to send a quote for the additional content!"

### Problem: Missed Deadlines

**Issue**: You can't deliver on time

**Solutions:**
1. Communicate EARLY (as soon as you know)
2. Offer options: extend deadline or partial refund
3. Provide progress update
4. Learn from it - set longer timeframes next time

**Communication Template:**
> "Hi! I want to give you an update on your custom request. I'm running a bit behind schedule due to [brief reason]. I can have this ready for you in 2 more days, or if you prefer, I can process a refund. I'm sorry for the delay and really appreciate your patience!"

### Problem: Fan Disputes Delivery

**Issue**: Fan says content doesn't match request

**Solutions:**
1. Review original request together
2. Show how you met requirements
3. Offer minor revisions if reasonable
4. Contact platform support if needed

**Prevention:**
- Screenshot original request
- Confirm details before creating
- Over-communicate during process
- Document everything
