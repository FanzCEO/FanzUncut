## Types of Promotions

### 1. Percentage Discounts

**How It Works:**
- Reduce subscription price by a percentage
- Applied at checkout
- Limited or unlimited uses

**Example:**
```
Regular Price: $25/month
50% OFF Promotion
Discounted Price: $12.50/month
Your Earnings: $10/month (80% of $12.50)
```

**Common Percentages:**
- **25% OFF**: Mild discount, good for loyal fans
- **50% OFF**: Popular new subscriber promotion
- **75% OFF**: Aggressive growth strategy
- **80%+ OFF**: First month only, very aggressive

---

### 2. Fixed Amount Discounts

**How It Works:**
- Reduce subscription by specific dollar amount
- Simple and clear messaging

**Example:**
```
Regular Price: $20/month
$10 OFF Promotion
Discounted Price: $10/month
Your Earnings: $8/month (80% of $10)
```

**Best For:**
- Clear, straightforward messaging
- Psychological pricing ($19.99 â†’ $9.99)
- Small, specific promotions

---

### 3. Free Trial Periods

**How It Works:**
- First X days free, then full price
- Automatic renewal at regular price
- Great for conversion

**Example:**
```
7-Day Free Trial
Then: $25/month
```

**Trial Lengths:**
- **3 days**: Very short, tests commitment
- **7 days**: Most popular, good balance
- **14 days**: Longer evaluation period
- **30 days**: Very generous, high conversion if content is strong

---

### 4. Limited-Time Offers

**How It Works:**
- Discount available for specific time period
- Creates urgency
- Expires automatically

**Example:**
```
50% OFF - 48 HOURS ONLY!
Ends: Sunday, Nov 19 at 11:59 PM
```

**Duration Options:**
- **24 hours**: Maximum urgency
- **48-72 hours**: Weekend special
- **7 days**: Week-long promotion
- **Monthly**: Recurring monthly deal

---

### 5. First Month Discounts

**How It Works:**
- Discount applies to first month only
- Automatically renews at full price
- Lowers barrier to entry

**Example:**
```
First Month: $5 (80% off)
Renewal: $25/month
```

**Strategy:**
- Hook them with low price
- Deliver massive value first month
- High conversion to full price

---

### 6. Bundle Deals

**How It Works:**
- Multiple months at discounted rate
- Prepaid upfront
- Guaranteed revenue

**Example:**
```
3-Month Bundle: $60 (normally $75)
Save 20%!
```

**Bundle Options:**
- **3 months**: 10-15% discount
- **6 months**: 20-25% discount
- **12 months**: 30-40% discount

---

### 7. Referral Discounts

**How It Works:**
- Current subscribers get discount for referring friends
- Friend gets discount too
- Win-win growth strategy

**Example:**
```
Refer a Friend:
You get: 1 month free
They get: 50% off first month
```

---

### 8. Loyalty Rewards

**How It Works:**
- Reward long-term subscribers
- Renewal discounts
- VIP pricing

**Example:**
```
Been subscribed 6+ months?
Get 25% off your renewal!
```

## Common Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Constant Discounting

**Problem:**
Running promotions year-round trains fans to never pay full price.

**Example:**
> "50% off always available" = Your actual price is 50% of stated

**Solution:**
- 70% of year at full price
- 30% of year with promotions
- Clear promotional windows
- Return to full price between

---

### Mistake 2: Deep Discounts, Poor Content

**Problem:**
Attracting subscribers with 80% off but not delivering value.

**Result:**
- Immediate cancellations
- Negative reviews
- Damaged reputation
- Wasted effort

**Solution:**
- Only promote when content is strong
- Ensure back catalog has value
- Deliver exceptional experience
- Under-promise, over-deliver

---

### Mistake 3: No Follow-Up

**Problem:**
Getting promotional subscribers but not engaging them.

**What Happens:**
- They forget why they subscribed
- No connection built
- Cancel after discount ends
- Wasted acquisition cost

**Solution:**
- Welcome message within 24 hours
- Engage frequently during discount period
- Ask for feedback
- Deliver premium value
- Build relationship
- 70%+ will stay at full price

---

### Mistake 4: Complicated Promotions

**Problem:**
"Get 30% off if you subscribe before Tuesday unless you're a returning subscriber in which case you get 40% off but only if you use code SAVE40 except on Wednesdays..."

**Result:**
- Confused potential subscribers
- Lost sales
- Support headaches

**Solution:**
Keep it simple:
> "50% OFF this weekend. Use code WEEKEND50. Ends Sunday."

---

### Mistake 5: Ignoring the Data

**Problem:**
Running same promotion repeatedly without checking if it works.

**Example:**
- "25% off" gets 5 signups
- "50% off" gets 50 signups
- Still running 25% off promotions

**Solution:**
- Track every promotion
- Compare results
- Double down on what works
- Stop what doesn't
- Continuously optimize
