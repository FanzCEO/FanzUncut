# Referral Programs

## Overview

Referral programs let you earn money by inviting new creators or fans to the platform. When someone you refer joins and succeeds, you get rewarded. It's a win-win: you help grow the platform community while earning passive income.

**Key Benefits:**
- **Passive Income**: Earn from referrals' activity
- **Help Others**: Share opportunity with friends
- **Build Network**: Grow your creator community
- **Compound Growth**: More referrals = more earnings
- **No Extra Work**: Earnings from their success

## Common Questions

### How much can I realistically earn?

**Conservative Estimate:**
```
5 active creator referrals
Each earning $300/month average
You earn 5% = $15/month per referral
Total: $75/month passive income
Year 1: $900
```

**Aggressive Estimate:**
```
25 active creator referrals
Each earning $800/month average
You earn 5% = $40/month per referral
Total: $1,000/month passive income
Year 1: $12,000
```

**Reality:**
- Most earn $50-$300/month from referrals
- Top referrers earn $1,000-$5,000/month
- Takes 6-12 months to build meaningful income
- Compounds over time

### Can I refer myself?

**NO:**
- Creating multiple accounts = violation
- Self-referrals = ban risk
- Gaming system = permanent removal
- You'll lose all earnings + main account

**Platform detects:**
- Same IP address
- Same payment info
- Same device
- Similar content/patterns

### What if my referral quits?

**Creator Referrals:**
- You stop earning when they stop earning
- Past commissions already paid (you keep)
- If they return, earnings resume
- No penalty to you

**Fan Referrals:**
- Bonus paid on first qualifying subscription
- You keep bonus even if they cancel
- One-time bonus per subscriber
- Encourage retention for better reputation

### Can I use paid ads?

**Generally YES, with restrictions:**

**Allowed:**
- âœ… Social media ads (Facebook, Instagram)
- âœ… Google Ads (with proper disclosures)
- âœ… Influencer partnerships
- âœ… Sponsored posts

**NOT Allowed:**
- âŒ Misleading claims
- âŒ False income promises
- âŒ Impersonating platform
- âŒ Trademark violations
- âŒ Adult ad networks (usually)

**Best Practice:**
- Check platform referral terms
- Use approved marketing materials
- Follow advertising regulations
- Disclose affiliate relationship
